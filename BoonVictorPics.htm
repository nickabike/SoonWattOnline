Pictures of Boon and Victor

At the 2009 OCBC Singapore Criterium

At the Singapore OCBC Singapore Criterium.  First time a race that attracted the participation of professional teams on an international level was promoted and held in Singapore.  Here we see Ah Boon on official duty.  

 Victor and Boon pose for a picture at the start line.  The race was a celebration with the cycling fraternity in Singapore.  This was at the Formula One Race paddocks where the race course routed through.  Victor Yew then, was the President of the Singapore Amatuer Cycling Association (SACA), now known as the Singapore Cycling Federation (SCF).  See an extract from the National Library Board of the SACA Archived Web Page and the new SCF Web Page here.  

When you meet them, you know that these bosses truly have a passion for cycling, sports and the bicycle business.
