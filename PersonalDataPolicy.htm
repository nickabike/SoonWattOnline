WEB BROWSER COOKIES

Cookies are usually a small text files with given ID tags that are stored on your computer, created for the purposes of enhancing user experience. Cookies are often indispensable for websites that have huge databases, require logins, and any other functions that help facilitate your browsing easier within that site. Due to the little amount of information a cookie contains, it usually cannot be used to reveal your identity or personally indentifying information.

The Customer may choose to disable the cookie function on their web browser. By doing so, please note that some parts of the this website may not function properly.
